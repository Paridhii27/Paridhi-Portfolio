Echoes

Touchdesigner | 2023

Echoes comes up in my mind a lot.
Echoes from a different place and time.
Echoes are memories, traces of a time gone by.
I think of time and what it means?
Time seems to move forward linearly but why is it that I find myself stuck in limbo,
stuck in memories.

This is a poem I wrote as I was making the visuals for this project and experimenting with type and form. Thinking of how echoes and sounds can be visually manifested and using noise and movement to translate the concept.
