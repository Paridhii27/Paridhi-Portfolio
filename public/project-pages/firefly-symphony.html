Firefly Symphony

Adobe Premier Pro | Blender | Touchdesigner | Arduino | Sculpting | Writing | 2025

Getting off the train, I hear a voice say "Welcome to the Firefly Symphony. The festival begins in five minutes as the sun begins its descent into the horizon. The weather's optimal outside, the sky a liminal space with pockets of moisture and the air pulsing with a luminous glow. Head over to the meadow with your firefly connectors."

Soon my firefly connector activates, pulsing rhythmically, the sensation tugging me northward. I walk through the meadow â€“ light bends, warps and sticks along firefly flight paths, transforming the space around me. Walking a mile, I become increasingly attuned to my surroundings. As I get closer to the fireflies, my connector dims to darkness which is a signal to let go. I slow my breathing and body's rhythm, becoming one with the ground and sky. Lying in the grass, I'm surrounded by the natural ephemeral glow of fireflies dancing through the air.
