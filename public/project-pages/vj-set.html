Audio Reactive

Generative visuals

Particle System

VJ set

Touchdesigner | Particle System |2023

Listening to music has always been my escape from reality. Music for me captures a feeling that goes beyond its lyrics and beats. It conjures up images in my head and feels like teleporting between entirely different worlds. Audio-reactive visuals intuitively enhance this experience, adding a dynamic, immersive layer that brings sound to life. This VJ set is based on the instrumental version of Stressed out by twenty-one pilots. The audio is analyzed to detect the snares and kicks in the sound. The visuals respond to these snares and kicks in many different ways such as the movement of shapes and the density of particles. A snare and kick gives a sudden boost to the visual while the audio spectrum is visualized by using 3D geometries, noise and particle systems.
