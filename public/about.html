Hi, I’m Paridhi — a creative technologist and experiential designer currently based in New York. I recently graduated with a Bachelor’s in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design. I’m interested in creating interactive experiences that encourage people to engage with their tactile surroundings, using technology as a bridge to deepen their connection to the physical world rather than distancing them from it.

I have a background in designing and developing interactive experiences which take various forms from experimental web interactions to playing with sensors in real time within physical spaces to create narrative driven immersive experiences. These experiences and digital products are designed to all in all bring people together and introduce them to creative ways of thinking and being.

While creating my projects I often find myself tinkering with various emerging technologies and ideating human machine interactions. I’ve previously collaborated with scientists from IBM Quantum and DLR Institute for AI safety and Security to create interactive exhibits to represent quantum computing.
