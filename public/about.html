Hi, I’m Paridhi Garg — a creative technologist, experiential designer, web designer and developer currently based in New York. I recently graduated with a degree in design and technology from Parsons School of Design. My work often finds itself at the intersections of human behaviors and memories as they inform digital interactions and experiences. Tinkering with all sorts of emerging technologies. Exploring speculative questions and fictional narratives relating the current world to an alternate existence. It’s alternate not in the sense that it’s removed from reality, rather as a space for asking why something is the way it is and as a means of drawing new connection between humans, nature and machines.

I’m interested in creating immersive interactive visuals and systems that encourage people to engage with their tactile surroundings and everyday experiences, using technology as a bridge to deepen their connection to the physical world rather than distancing them from it.
